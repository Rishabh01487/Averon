Built for the Asset Economy

Three integrated layers — tokenization, financing, and capital mobility — in one protocol.

⛓️

Asset Tokenization

Transform any verified real-world asset into programmable fractional tokens on a custom PoW blockchain with SHA-256 mining and ECDSA wallets.

🤖

AI-Powered Verification

A five-stage AI pipeline using Gemini 2.0 Flash analyzes your documents for legitimacy, valuation, and risk — reducing onboarding from days to minutes.

💰

Asset-Backed Financing

Unlock liquidity without selling your assets. Borrowers receive fiat (INR); investors participate through Averon Coin. Event-driven repayment, no lock-in.

🔄

Capital Mobility Network

Returned capital is automatically redeployed to new verified opportunities through risk-tiered liquidity pools — a continuously compounding economy.

🔒

Escrow & Compliance

On-chain escrow with atomic token claiming, four-tier KYC, eight-flag AML screening, and tamper-proof hash-chained audit logging.

📊

Trading & Price Discovery

A centralized order-book exchange with price-time priority matching, circuit breaker protection, and a dual-factor algorithmic pricing model.
